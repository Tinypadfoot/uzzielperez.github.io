Particle Hunting Map: HEP Theory & Phenomenology

My personal notes on Theoretical Particle Physics.

They say, the best way to learn something is to explain it to someone else. I'm going to populate this site as I finish my classes in High Energy Physics (Standard Model) and Beyond the Standard Model. I'll also document how to use Monte Carlo Generators like Sherpa and MadGraph and tie how they work with my understanding of the physics we're calculating.

Popular science books are worth the attention. We'll take a look at some of them here too (Keys: Brian Greene, Stephen Hawking, Sean Carroll, and Lisa Randall).
